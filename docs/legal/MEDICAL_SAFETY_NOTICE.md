# Medical and Safety Notice

## Emergency Situations

Sturdy is not designed for emergencies.

If a child is experiencing a medical emergency, injury, or immediate danger, contact your local emergency services immediately.

Examples of emergencies include:

- difficulty breathing
- loss of consciousness
- serious injury
- severe allergic reactions
- threats of self-harm

In these situations, professional medical assistance should be sought immediately.

---

## Health and Development Concerns

If you have concerns about your child's:

- physical health
- mental health
- developmental progress
- behavioral challenges

you should consult a qualified healthcare professional.

---

## Parenting Support Only

Sturdy provides general parenting support and practical communication scripts.

It does not diagnose or treat medical or psychological conditions.

The app should not be used as a substitute for professional healthcare advice or services.

# Privacy Policy

## Overview

Sturdy respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the Sturdy application and how it may be used.

---

## Information We Collect

Sturdy may collect the following types of information:

### Account Information

- email address
- authentication identifiers

### Child Profile Information

Parents may optionally provide information about their child such as:

- name
- age range
- preferences or behavioral patterns

This information is used solely to personalize parenting guidance.

---

## Usage Information

Sturdy may collect information about how the app is used, including:

- situations described by parents
- scripts generated
- feedback on whether guidance was helpful

This information helps improve the quality of responses over time.

---

## Data Storage

Data is stored securely using modern cloud infrastructure.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data from unauthorized access.

---

## Data Control

Parents can:

- edit child profiles
- delete stored information
- remove saved scripts

Data removal requests can be made through the application or by contacting support.

---

## Third-Party Services

Sturdy may use third-party services for:

- authentication
- analytics
- cloud infrastructure
- AI processing

These services are used only to operate and improve the product.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically.

Users will be notified of significant changes when appropriate.

# AI Limitations

## Overview

Sturdy uses artificial intelligence to generate supportive parenting guidance and practical scripts.

While the system is designed to provide helpful suggestions, AI-generated responses may not always be accurate, complete, or appropriate for every situation.

Parents should always use their own judgment when deciding how to respond to their child.

---

## Not Professional Advice

Sturdy does not provide:

- medical advice
- psychological treatment
- therapy
- clinical diagnosis

The information provided by Sturdy is intended only as general parenting support.

Parents should consult qualified professionals for medical, psychological, or developmental concerns.

---

## AI Response Limitations

AI-generated responses may sometimes:

- misunderstand the situation described
- produce incomplete guidance
- suggest approaches that may not work for every child

Parenting situations vary widely, and no automated system can fully understand every family context.

---

## Parent Responsibility

Parents remain responsible for all decisions made when interacting with their child.

Sturdy is intended to support parents with ideas and scripts, but it cannot replace a parent’s judgment or experience.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Sturdy is continually being improved.

Feedback from users helps refine the system so that guidance becomes more helpful over time.

However, AI responses should always be treated as supportive suggestions, not definitive instructions.
